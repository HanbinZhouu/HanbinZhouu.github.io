Research Focus

PhD student at Xi'an Jiaotong University specializing in AI-driven industrial systems. My work bridges mechanical engineering with cutting-edge AI, focusing on:

  • 3D Vision & Robotics: Industrial inspection systems, gear measurement, point cloud processing
  • Intelligent Maintenance: Fault diagnosis using Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Adaptive Systems: Transfer learning for cross-domain industrial applications

Previously developed an aviation gear database (6000+ images, 1000+ point clouds) and published in Measurement Science and Technology.
